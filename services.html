Beyond The Box

We don't just sell gadgets — we make sure they actually work in your loved one's life. Here's everything we do to support you.

📦

Pre-Setup & Pre-Activation

Every device ships pre-charged, pre-configured, and pre-paired. Your loved one plugs it in and it works — no SIM cards, no logins, no accounts.

📞

24/7 Live Phone Support

US-based human support, average pickup time under 90 seconds. We patiently walk anyone — at any age — through any question.

Free Video Setup Help

Schedule a 1:1 video call with a tech advisor who'll guide your parent or grandparent through setup, step-by-step.

Free weekly group classes covering smartphones, video calling, online safety, and more — taught by patient instructors.
