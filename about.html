Technology That Respects Age

We build gadgets we'd give to our own parents and grandparents — designed with patience, tested for clarity, backed by real human support.

Our Story

SilverTech started in 2019 when our founder watched her 78-year-old father struggle with a smartphone he received as a gift. The buttons were too small, the menus too deep, and the help line led to an automated maze. She knew there had to be a better way.

Five years later, we've helped over 25,000 American families equip their loved ones with technology that actually works for them. Every product we sell is hand-tested by our team of seniors aged 65 to 91 — if they can't set it up in 10 minutes, we don't carry it.

Our Mission

To make modern technology genuinely accessible for older adults — not by dumbing it down, but by designing it with the dignity and clarity it deserves.

What Makes Us Different

  • Senior-first design: Every product is reviewed by our 12-person senior advisory panel before we add it to the store.
  • Real humans, real fast: Our US-based support team picks up in under 90 seconds — no menus, no scripts.
  • Two-year promise: Every gadget is covered by our 2-year repair-or-replace warranty.
  • Family setup help: We walk grandkids and caregivers through device setup over video — free.
